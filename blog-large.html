OUR MISSION

K.VG. Amarajyothi P U College, believes that every individual from each strata of society needs affordable, relevant and quality education to fulfill personal aspirations. The vision of the Institution is to constantly strive towards meeting the social needs by inclusion and expansion of newer streams of teaching methods in its Institutions and provide world-class infrastructure for learning. In fulfillment of its vision the Institution commits itself to a mission to exceed in all its activities to create the atmosphere of effective learning, generate a spirit of enquiry and healthy challenges and competitions, encourage sustainable accomplishments and ensure enriching rewards for everyone and the society at large.
